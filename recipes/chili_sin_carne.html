Chili sin Carne

Warm up with a hearty bowl of Chili sin Carne—a savory plant-based chili loaded with fiber, protein, and antioxidants. This meatless wonder boasts remarkable nutritional benefits, making it an ideal choice for nourishing your body and powering your day.

Fuel your body intelligently with Chili sin Carne, and experience the nutritional benefits that will keep you feeling satisfied. Whether you're an athlete, fitness enthusiast, or simply craving a wholesome and delicious meal, this fiber-filled chili promises a nutritious and mouthwatering dish.

  • Sustained Energy Release: The fiber, protein, and complex carbs in this chili offer a slow-burning source of energy to power you through your day.
  • Muscle Repair and Satiety Support: The plant-based protein and fiber promote satiety and support muscle recovery when enjoyed after a workout.
  • Nutrient-Dense Ingredients: Packed with protein, antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals from beans, tomatoes, peppers, and spices. Easily customized to your tastes and nutrition needs.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • 1 Avocado slices (for garnish)
  • 1 Vegan sour cream (optional, for serving)
  • 250 g Cooked rice or tortilla chips (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté for 5 minutes until translucent.
  2. Add the garlic and bell pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the beans, tomatoes, tomato paste, vegetable broth, chili powder, oregano, salt, pepper, and lime juice. Stir to combine.
  4. Bring the chili to a boil. Then reduce heat and let simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed. Add more chili powder for a spicier chili or lime juice to brighten the flavors.
  6. Serve the chili hot, garnished with cilantro, avocado slices, and desired toppings like vegan sour cream or tortilla chips.

Enjoy this hearty meatless chili! Feel free to add your own spin with different beans, vegetables, or spices.
