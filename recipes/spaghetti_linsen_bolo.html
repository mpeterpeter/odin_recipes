Spaghetti with Lentil-Bolognese

Enjoy a hearty, nutritious and protein-packed pasta dish with this Spaghetti with Lentil Bolognese. This plant-based twist on a classic Italian favorite packs a flavorful punch along with remarkable nutritional benefits. Make this your new go-to meal for a satisfying dinner that will power you through the rest of your day.

Fuel your active lifestyle with the sustained energy release from protein-rich lentils, diced vegetables and savory Italian herbs. The tomato base delivers a boost of antioxidants, while the red wine adds depth of flavor. Customize it to your taste by adding extra vegetables or spices.

  • Protein-Packed: Lentils deliver a powerful protein punch to help repair muscles after tough workouts.
  • Sustained Energy: Complex carbs from the pasta combined with protein and fiber from the lentils provide steady, long-lasting energy.
  • Nutrient-Dense: Packed with protein, fiber, antioxidants, vitamins and minerals to nourish an active body.
  • Plant-Powered: A hearty, satisfying meatless meal perfect for health-conscious athletes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils, rinsed and drained
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary (crushed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • 500 g Cooked pasta of your choice

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Lentils: Rinse and drain the lentils. In a medium pot, combine the lentils with 4 cups water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer for 20-25 minutes, until lentils are tender but still hold their shape. Drain any excess water.
  2. Make the Sauce: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and saut√© for 3-4 minutes until translucent. Add the garlic, carrot, celery and bell pepper. Cook for 5 more minutes.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, thyme, rosemary, salt, pepper and red wine (if using). Stir to combine.
  4. Add the cooked lentils and bay leaf. Let simmer for 15-20 minutes, until thickened.
  5. Remove bay leaf. Stir in chopped parsley.
  6. Serve lentil bolognese over cooked pasta. Garnish with more parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Enjoy your Lentil Bolognese! Feel free to customize the recipe with additional vegetables, herbs, or spices to suit your taste.
